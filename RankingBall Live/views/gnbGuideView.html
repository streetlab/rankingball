Fantasy Premier League

CONTEST RULES

  • In each contest, participants will be assigned a fixed salary cap of $35,000 that they can use to draft their 8-player roster.
  • The Player Pool will consist of all league players expected to be playing on a given game day. Any missing players will not be added to the pool once fantasy football live starts offering contests for that day. Player salaries will also not change once fantasy football live starts offering contests for that day.
  • Contest results and winners will be based on the total points scored across each entrant's 8-player roster (scoring summarized below).
  • Participation in each contest must be made only as specified in the Terms of Use. Failure to comply with these Terms of Use will result in disqualification and, if applicable, prize forfeiture.

TEAM ROSTERS

  • Rosters will consist of 8 players and must include players from at least 2 different teams.
  • The 8 roster positions are: GK, DF, DF, MF, MF, FW, FW, FREE (any non-goalie)

POINT SCORING

  • Players will accumulate points as follows:
    1. Goal = +1,000 PTs
    2. Assist = +600 PTs
    3. Shot = +100 PTs
    4. Shot on Goal = +100 PTs
    5. Crosses = +50 PTs (via corner kick, open play, and set pieces)
    6. Fouls Conceded = -100 PTs
    7. Yellow Card = -100 PTs
    8. Red Card = -100 PTs
    9. Saves (GK) = +300 PTs
    10. Clean Sheet = +200 PTs
    11. Goal Allowed = -200 PTs
    12. Win = +500 PTs

Realtime Challenge

Real Time Challenge?

Real Time Challenge is a prediction game which team will make shot in upcoming next minute.
For example, you expect a shot by team A at 9 minute during a match, you press team A button at 8 minute before it happen.
You will get points if the prediction is correct.

How to play

  1. Select a team you expect to make a shot next minute. If a team is selected between 8’00’’ and 8’59’’ in a match, it means that your targeted time is from 9’00’’ to 9’59’’.
  2. Each attempt consumes a Challenge Ball which is recharged every 3 minutes. Challenge Balls are recharged no more than 3.
  3. Wait result. Your attempt time and team are displayed on screen.

    ① Selected team
    ② Targeted time
    ③ Cool down till next attempt
  4. 4. If your attempt was successful, you would obtain points by situation:
    Goal (+5)
    Shot on target (+3)
    Shot off target (+1)
  5. 5. Fill up 10 points and get a reward.

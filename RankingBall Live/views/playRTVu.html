Real Time Challenge?

Real Time Challenge is a prediction game which team will make shot in upcoming next minute.
For example, you expect a shot by team A at 9 minute during a match, you press team A button at 8 minute before it happen.
You will get points if the prediction is correct.

How to play

  1. Select a team you expect to make a shot next minute. If a team is selected between 8’00’’ and 8’59’’ in a match, it means that your targeted time is from 9’00’’ to 9’59’’.
  2. Each attempt consumes a Challenge Ball which is recharged every 3 minutes. Challenge Balls are recharged no more than 3.
  3. Wait result. Your attempt time and team are displayed on screen.

    ① Selected team
    ② Targeted time
    ③ Cool down till next attempt
  4. 4. If your attempt was successful, you would obtain points by situation:
    Goal (+5)
    Shot on target (+3)
    Shot off target (+1)
  5. 5. Fill up 10 points and get a reward.
